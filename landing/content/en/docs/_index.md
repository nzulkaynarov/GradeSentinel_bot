<section class="doc-section">
  <h2>Quick start</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Open <code>@GradeSentinel_bot</code> in Telegram and press <span class="kbd">START</span>.</li>
    <li>Pick your language and share your phone number.</li>
    <li>Create a family, add a child via a Google Sheets link, invite relatives.</li>
    <li>Pick a plan with <code>/subscription</code>.</li>
  </ol>
